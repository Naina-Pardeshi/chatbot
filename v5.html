*How to Download Covid-19 Vaccination Certificate from Cowin?


Step 1: Visit the professional internet site of Cowin i.e. https://www.cowin.gov.in/

Step 3: Sign in the use of your registered cell wide variety after which input the one-time password or OTP acquired on that wide variety.
Step 4: Once you log in, there can be a Certificate tab beneath neath your name.
Step 5: Click the download button to get the gentle replica of your vaccination certificate.

*How to Download Covid-19 Vaccination Certificate from the Aarogya Setu app?


Step 1: Open the app on your phone (You can download the Aarogya Setu app from Google Play Store or Apple App Store if it is not already installed)
Step 2: Log in with your mobile number and click on the Cowin tab at the top.
Step 3: Enter your 13 digit beneficiary reference ID after clicking the Vaccination certificate option
Step 4: Click the download button to get the vaccination certificate

*How to download Cowin Vaccination Certificate from Digilocker App?


Step 1: Visit the Play Store to download and install the DigiLocker app if you haven’t already.
Step 2: Now go through the registration process in the app by entering details such as name, date of birth, gender, security PIN, cell phone number, Aadhar number, and phone number. messaging.
Step 3: Now after registering go to the Central Government tab and click on Ministry of Family Health and Welfare (MoHFW).
Step 4:You will see an option labeled “Vaccine Certified”.
Step 5: Click on the Vaccine Certificate link and enter your 13-digit Reference ID to download the Covid Vaccine Certificate.

*How to download Corona Vaccine Certificate From Umang App


Step 1: Using the Umang app and downloading the Covid vaccine certificate is really easy. See these steps to get the process of downloading vaccination certificates from the UMANG app.
Step 2: Open the UMANG application. If you haven’t downloaded the app yet, go to the Play Store and download the app from there.
Step 3:Open the app on your phone and find the “What’s New” section.
Step 4:In the “What’s New” section, you will find a tab called Cowin.
Step 5: Click on Cowin and press the Download vaccination certificate option.
Step 6: Now enter your mobile number and also enter the OTP which has just been sent to your mobile.
