Steps to follow for vaccination.

Vaccine Registration Video

Steps:

1)Register online through Co-Win or Aarogya Setu or Umang platform. (You could refer above vaccine registration video for this step)

2)Register against only on phone number and one proof of ID

3)Carry ID proof when you go for registration.

4)Search for nearest Vaccination center online and select center convenient for vaccination.

5)To receive Health ID as a part of registration for vaccination ,give your consent.

6)Reach Vaccination centre on the specified date and time.

7)Wait for 30 minutes at the vaccination centre after vaccination, inform if any side effect(s) is experienced within 30 minutes.

8)Inform Helpine Number: +91-11-23978046 (Toll Free- 1075) incase of any side effect after leaving vaccination centre.

9)Continue precautions of social distancing and wearing masks.
