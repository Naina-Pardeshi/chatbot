About Us

Our Mission

At the heart of it all, Helping-Hands philosophy and initiatives are to serve as a platform where all queries of an individual can be solved.Helping-Hand aims at reducing stress on government and healthcare organizations.

During pandemic,When there are No reliable sources of information a lot of rumors are spread and this also leads to spread of misinformation which in turn creates an atmosphere of panic and anxiety.Absence of concise information from credible sources lead people in overwhelming social media search or web search engines long list of individuals fears that disclose symptoms which can cause unnecessary trauma,depression and panic.Therefore our website serves as a one stop destination which provides all the information on Covid-19 and a platform where one can feel free to solve their doubts.
